## Game flow

Experience a complete daily cycle of Tokyo commuter life:

1. **Morning preparation:** Start your day in Yokohama with your daily budget
2. **Convenience store shopping:** Buy breakfast, drinks, and weather protection items
3. **Train selection:** Choose your train type balancing cost, speed, and energy consumption
4. **Commute to Tokyo:** Navigate social encounters, random events, and train observations
5. **Work day:** Experience automatic lunch deduction and work phases
6. **Evening commute:** Handle the challenges of rush hour and optional team events
7. **Daily summary:** Receive performance feedback and grading based on your choices

## Why play Into Tokyo?

- **Cultural authenticity:** Experience realistic Japanese commuter culture and social etiquette
- **Strategic gameplay:** Every decision matters - from train selection to convenience store purchases
- **Immersive simulation:** Dynamic weather, social encounters, and realistic train fare systems
- **Educational value:** Learn about Tokyo's train system and Japanese daily life

### Frequently asked questions

**Q: How do I start playing?**

**A:** Download Into Tokyo from the App Store and begin your commuter simulation! Start in Yokohama with ¬•2,000 and make strategic choices throughout your day.

**Q: What happens if I run out of energy or money?**

**A:** The game ends based on various conditions including running out of energy, money, or making poor social choices. You'll receive feedback and can start a new day.

**Q: Are there different difficulty levels?**

**A:** The game features natural difficulty through your choices - train types, weather conditions, and social encounters all affect the challenge level.

**Q: Can I track my progress over multiple days?**

**A:** Yes, the app includes comprehensive game history and statistics to track your performance across multiple commute attempts.
